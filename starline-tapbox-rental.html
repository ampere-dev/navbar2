Starline Busway TapBoxes for "U" shaped open channel busway track systems

These are used to provide a connection system for overhead power distribution networks, so that a load bank and cable can be used to comissioning the busway and verify operation.

These are only compatible with the 400T5 series busway, and rated for 600V 400A.
