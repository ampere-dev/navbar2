CAPABILITY STATEMENT

Designed - Innovative - Dynamic


Ampere.dev is a startup company that derived from the frustration of seeing rental customers being subjected to poor equipment maintenance, lack of knowledge from sales personnel, a disregard for support and gross neglect on project awareness from equipment providers. Ampere provides consulting on load banks, commissioning projects, power quality studies, battery systems, transformers, IR inspections, switchgear, power cables and overall quality control. We love challenges and thrive on making the improbable, possible and being in the industry since 1999, have seen basically everything one can dream up. We like to think of solutions to problems we haven't seen yet.
